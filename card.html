NASI LEMAK

Description

Nasi Lemak is Malaysia's national dish, featuring fragrant rice cooked in coconut milk, served with sambal, anchovies, peanuts, boiled egg, and cucumber. This flavorful meal is enjoyed at any time of day and is traditionally wrapped in a banana leaf. Its rich, creamy rice paired with spicy sambal and crunchy sides creates a delightful blend of textures and flavors. Variations may include fried chicken, rendang, or sambal squid.

Allergens

  • Peanuts
  • Anchovies
  • Eggs
